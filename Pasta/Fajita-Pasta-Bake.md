# Fajita Pasta Bake

## Ingredients
• 1 yellow bell pepper, seeded and sliced  
• 1 green bell pepper, seeded and sliced  
• 1 red bell pepper, seeded and sliced  
• 2.5 cups mushroom, sliced  
• 1 medium yellow onion, diced  

• 1 tablespoon chili powder  
• 1 tablespoon paprika  
• 1 tablespoon garlic  
• 1 tablespoon cumin  
• 1 teaspoon salt  
• 1 teaspoon pepper  

• 3 tablespoons olive oil  

• 4 cups penne, uncooked  

• 1.5 cups sour cream  
• 3 cups shredded pepper jack cheese  
  
• fresh parsley (garnish)  

## Steps
1. Preheat over to 400 degrees F  
2. In a nonstick baking dish, add  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 sliced yellow bell pepper  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 sliced red bell pepper  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 sliced green bell pepper  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 2.5 cups sliced mushroom  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 diced medium yellow onion  
3. In a small bowl combine  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 tbsp chili powder  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 tbsp paprika  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 tbsp garlic  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 tbsp cumin  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 tsp salt  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1 tsp pepper  
4. Pour 3 tbsp olive oil and half of the spice mix over the vegetables and toss well to coat  
5. Bake vegetables for about 30 mins, stirring occasionally, until tender  
6. In a large pot of boiling water, cook 4 cups penne according to package instructions  
7. Drain pasta, but safe 1 cup pasta water
8. Return drained pasta to the pot and add, mix to combine  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * The roasted vegetables  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * The rest of the spice mix  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * The 1 cup pasta water  
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; * 1.5 cups sour cream  
9. Transfer pasta mixture to the baking dish used for roasting veggies and spread evenly. Sprinkle 3 cups pepper jack over the top  
10. Bake for about 15 mins, until cheese is golden brown  
11. Let cool for about 5 mins, garnish with parsley if desired, and serve
